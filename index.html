Academic Bio

I am currently pursuing full-time doctoral research under the guidance of Dr. Syed Umarhathab, Assistant Professor and Head of the Department of Criminology and Criminal Justice, Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli.

My academic profile is supported by a solid foundation: a B.A. in Criminology and Police Administration from J.H.A. Agarsen College (University of Madras) with 79.3%, an M.Sc. in Criminology and Criminal Justice Sciences from MSU with 86.8%, and a technical Honours Diploma in Computer Applications (76%). I recently qualified the **UGC NET** for Ph.D. Admission (June & Dec 2024, 2025).

Awards & Fellowships: I am honored to be a BPR&D Junior Research Fellow (officially selected for the prestigious Government of India (GoI) Fellowship Scheme to conduct doctoral research on Criminology, Police Science, and related subjects) and the **MGR Fellowship Awardee, 2025**. I was also awarded the prestigious Young Criminologist Award by the Indian Society of Criminology (2026, Navi Mumbai) and the Professor Dr. P. Madhava Soma Sundaram Best Research Paper Award (2023).

Research Grants & Projects: I serve as the Principal Investigator for a substantial research project sanctioned by the Tamil Nadu Government's Chief Minister's Research Grant (CMRG) with a total grant funding of 19.29 Lakh.

Leadership & Extra-Curriculars: I actively participated in the Republic Day Parade in Tamil Nadu for two consecutive years (2019 and 2020) and was elected as an Executive Member of the Criminal Justice Forum (CJF) for 2021-2023. I have also served as a Drill Instructor and Camp Officer for NSS Special Camps, driving student character development and civic vigilance.

Journal Article 2026

Global Research Trends in 5G Technology: A Bibliometric Analysis with Emerging Directions Towards 5G-Advanced and 6G

Published in the International Journal of Innovative Science and Research Technology. A comprehensive bibliometric analysis mapping the research landscape and emerging development corridors for 5G, 5G-Advanced, and 6G.

Background & Abstract

The exponential growth of fifth-generation (5G) wireless technology has generated an unprecedented research footprint. This study maps the developmental trajectories, citation hotspots, and global publication clusters of 5G technologies, detailing the transitioning corridors towards 5G-Advanced and future 6G communication frameworks.

Contributor: AKASH J | Publisher: IJISRT | Year: 2026

Journal Article 2025

THE AMBIT OF LAW IN RESPONDING TO SOCIAL ANXIETY AND DEVIANT BEHAVIOUR AMONG CHILDREN: A STUDY OF SMARTPHONE ADDICTION AND CYBER VICTIMIZATION

Published in the Indian Journal of Law and Legal Research. Reviews legal frameworks protecting minors against deviant behaviors and cyber victimization hazards arising from smartphone dependencies.

Background & Abstract

The rapid proliferation of smartphones and digital technologies has significantly transformed children's lives. This study evaluates the increasing vulnerabilities of minors to social anxiety and behavioral deviances. We highlight the urgent need for integrated legal strategies in India to safeguard children's rights and promote responsible digital habits.

Akash. J, Dr. Syed Umarhathab | Publisher: IJLLR (Vol-VII, Issue-II) | ISSN: 2582-8878 | Year: 2025
