Academic Bio

I am currently pursuing full-time doctoral research under the guidance of Dr. Syed Umarhathab, Assistant Professor and Head of the Department of Criminology and Criminal Justice, Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli.

My academic profile is supported by a solid foundation: a B.A. in Criminology and Police Administration from the University of Madras, an M.Sc. in Criminology and Criminal Justice Sciences from MSU, and a technical Honours Diploma in Computer Applications. I specialize in Biosocial Criminology, child justice, penology, and digital threat landscapes.

Awards & Fellowships: I am honored to be a BPR&D Junior Research Fellow (awarded by the Bureau of Police Research and Development, Government of India) and a recipient of the MGR Fellowship. On 21st March 2023, I was awarded the prestigious Professor Dr. P. Madhava Soma Sundaram Best Research Paper Award by the Department of Criminology and Criminal Justice, MSU.

Leadership & Extra-Curriculars: I actively participated in the Republic Day Parade in Tamil Nadu for two consecutive years (2019 and 2020) and was elected as an Executive Member of the Criminal Justice Forum (CJF) for 2021-2022. I have also served as a Drill Instructor and Camp Officer for NSS Special Camps, driving student character development and civic vigilance.
